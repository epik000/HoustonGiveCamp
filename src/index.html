6:30 pm

Charity Meet and Greet

Two week prior to the coding weekend we all get together for a meet and greet, Dev's and Charities. (Notice it's on Wednesday to respect ACL!)

Charities will each give a 2 minute presentation about who they are and what they hope to get out of the GiveCamp project. This will take maybe 1/2 hr. We will also hand out one-page developer-written technical summaries of the projects.

Then, during the next half hour, developers get time to chat with the charities, review the summaries, and select their charity preferences.

We'll provide snacks and appetizers! :)
